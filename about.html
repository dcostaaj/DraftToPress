About Us

Critique corner is a website where you can come to talk about your favourite books, songs, albums or movies. It's also a place to read opinion pieces and personal essays. Critique corner is all about sharing personal thoughts about different media. Together our team values creativity and having the freedom to speak your mind. Together we aim to bring the storytellers of the world together.
